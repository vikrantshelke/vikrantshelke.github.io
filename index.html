About me

Engineering graduate with Masters in Data Analytics at Northeastern, I often find myself juggling interests that range from coding and building data pipelines to photography, brewing good coffee, and nerding out over superhero stories and poetry.

With a foundation in Data Engineering and a heart captivated by the endless possibilities of technology, I, Vikrant Manohar Shelke, navigate the vast ocean of data with precision and creativity.

My professional voyage commenced at Infosys, where I mastered the art of sculpting SSIS packages and SQL queries, fostering seamless data integrations and powering business intelligence breakthroughs. An enriching internship at The Sparks Foundation deepened my dive into Data Science and Business Analysis, unlocking data-driven strategies for enhanced profitability and decision-making.

Currently, my quest for knowledge has led me to the realms of AWS & Databricks, where I'm expanding my expertise to pioneer in sustainable and impactful tech solutions. Beyond the bits and bytes, I'm a relentless learner and a tech enthusiast eager to connect and share this journey of discovery and innovation. Let's embark on this data-driven adventure together.
